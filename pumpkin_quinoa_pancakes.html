Pumpkin Quinoa Pancakes

Description

This is a recipe for pumpkin quinoa pancakes. Why quinoa? I don't know! But I'd be willing to try. Below is the recipe for this tasty autumnal treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups quinoa flour
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground allspice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 3/4 cups buttermilk
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs vegetable oil
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Mix quinoa flour, brown sugar, baking soda, cinnamon, allspice, salt, and ginger together in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk buttermilk, pumpkin puree, eggs, and oil together in a separate bowl. Pour over quinoa flour mixture; stir just until batter is blended.
  3. Preheat a large nonstick skillet over medium heat; spray with cooking spray. Pour 1/4 cup batter into the skillet. Cook until bubbles form and edges are dry, 3 to 4 minutes. Flip and continue cooking until the center springs back when pressed, 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Repeat with remaining batter.
