Strawberry Smoothie

Description

This is a recipe for a strawberry smoothie. Smoothies make excellent pre-workout snacks, and their cleanup is always easy. I haven't tried this one, but I might after making this page.

Ingredients

  • 8 hulled strawberries
  • 1/2 cup skim milk
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 6 cubes of crushed ice

Steps

  1. Combine strawberries, milk, yogurt, sugar, and vanilla in a blender. Add ice and blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
